About HackMoL2.0

HackMol2.0 is a community - focused 36 Hours Long Virtual Hackathon organized by DSC NIT Jalandhar, designed especially for the needs of the community itself. Whether you are a beginner or expert ,here is a perfect chance to show your skills and witness a competitive yet inclusive developer community around it.

In light of the Covid-19 outbreak, your safety and well being are our primary concern. Hence all the activities Related to HackMol2.0 like workshops,talks will be hosted Virtually.

The Hackathon is purely based on Open Innovation, teams can openly pick up any tracks, themes and problem statement.

Rules

1

Each team must contain between 2 - 5 members.

2

The Project must be build entirely during the Duration of the Hackathon

3

Each participant must adhere to our Code of Conduct.

4

Hackers can work on any technical domain and idea of their choice. Problem Ideas will also be provided during ideation event.

5

For precise details head to here to our Devfolio Page.

6

Hackathon and Events are Open to and free for Student Developers.

7

All the submissions will be reviewed by the organizing committee & fixed top submissions will qualify for review by a panel of judges. Finalist teams may be called for special presentation with the Judges.

8

Organizers Reserves the final right of decision in any case and Rules Regulations can be changed at any point of time.

What is a Hackathon and who can Participate in HackMol2.0 ?

A Hackathon is an intense event that brings together designers and developers along with industry experts to identify problems and create software solutions, usually within 24-48 hours. If you're a student developer, we'd love to have you at HacMol2.0! Whether that's a undergrad, or graduate, we believe you can get something out of the event.

What can be the size of Team?

A team can consist of 2-5 members. Pro Tip: Aim to have a mix of people with both design and developer skills.

How much does it Cost ?

Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. Admission to HackWol2.0 is completely free for all student Developers.

What if I don't have a team or Idea ?

Don't worry, most people dont! Well have team formation and ideation events geared towards helping you find people to work with. Its perfectly normal to go around to people, tell them youre looking for a team, and ask if you can help out. You can also show yourself off at #teamup on our Discord Server.
